Texas COVID-19 School District Monitoring Dashboard

Apr 27, 2020
Media

The Texas School District COVID-19 Monitoring Dashboard is a dashboard built by my team at the Texas Policy Lab to help Texas policymakers, school districts, and parents of school-age students make better and more informed decisions about COVID-19 mitigation during the pandemic. The dashboard was developed based on guidance from the Centers for Disease Control (CDC) and tracks several standardized metrics, including case numbers, test positivity rates, and hospital capacity, and transmission risk levels within counties and school districts.
